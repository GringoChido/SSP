Our Story

Second Son Productions started from a real love of this music. Not the industry side of it — the actual music. The late-night sessions, the live shows where the whole room is locked in, the moment when an artist finds that thing that only they can do.

We built this company around one idea: artists do their best work when they've got a team that actually gets it. Not a corporate machine, but real people who understand the culture, respect the craft, and handle the business so our artists never have to choose between making great art and making a living.

Our family includes Robert Glasper, Lalah Hathaway, Chief Xian aTunde Adjuah, Derrick Hodge, Kendrick Scott, Taylor McFerrin, and Elena Pinderhughes. Between them, they've got twelve Grammys, hundreds of albums, and have played pretty much every stage that matters. But what makes them special isn't the accolades — it's the fact that they're still pushing, still experimenting, still making music that surprises people.
