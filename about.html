The Collective

Second Son Productions is a management and production firm built around a singular conviction: the most consequential artists require infrastructure as ambitious as their vision. We architect careers across three verticals: strategic management, film and television composition, and global live events. This ensures our roster operates at the intersection of artistic integrity and commercial reach.

Our collective includes Robert Glasper, Lalah Hathaway, Chief Adjuah, Derrick Hodge, Kendrick Scott, Taylor McFerrin, and Elena Pinderhughes. Twelve Grammy wins, orchestral commissions from the Super Bowl to the Academy Awards, and a catalog that has fundamentally altered the relationship between jazz, R&B, hip-hop, and electronic music. We don’t manage genres. We manage legacies.

Our Practice

Management & Strategy

Long-term career architecture for artists who operate beyond category. We handle strategy, brand positioning, and deal structure so our clients stay focused on the work that moves culture.

Composition & Orchestration

Our roster scores, orchestrates, and conducts for film, television, and live broadcast at the highest level. From the Academy Awards to the Super Bowl, we place the right musical mind on every brief.

Festival Programming & Live Events

We curate and produce flagship live experiences, from the Blue Note Jazz Festival at the Hollywood Bowl to the Black Radio Experience. Turnkey programming built on cultural credibility and operational precision.
